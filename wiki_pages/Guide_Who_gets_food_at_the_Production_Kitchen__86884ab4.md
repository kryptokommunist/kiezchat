# 🍽️ Production Kitchen Food Guide

Welcome to the Production Kitchen!

Our kitchen exists to support the people who help build, run, and dismantle the festival. To keep things fair and sustainable, please follow the guidelines below.


---

## 🔨 During Build & Strike

### Volunteers

All registered volunteers receive meals during their scheduled shifts.

**Early Shift**

* Breakfast
* Lunch

**Late Shift**

* Lunch
* Dinner

### Realizers

All Realizers receive all meals throughout the entire Build and Strike period.

### Kitchen Volunteers

If you are working a kitchen shift, you are always welcome to eat the meal that is being prepared and served during your shift.


---

## 🔥 During the Burn

The Production Kitchen is reserved for operational staff who keep the event running safely.

### Meals are provided for:

* Security
* First Aid
* Kitchen Volunteers during their kitchen shift

### Welfare & Rangers

Volunteers on late Welfare or Ranger shifts may receive meal vouchers according to their department's arrangements.


---

## ❤️ A Few Friendly Notes

* Please only take meals you are entitled to so we can make sure everyone is fed.
* If you are unsure whether you qualify for a meal, ask the Kitchen Lead before serving yourself.
* Help us reduce food waste by taking only what you will eat.
* Be kind to the kitchen crew — they are volunteers and staff working hard to keep everyone nourished.

Thank you for helping make this event possible. Enjoy your meal and take care of each other! 🍲✨
