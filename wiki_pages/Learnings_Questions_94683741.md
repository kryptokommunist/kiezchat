# Learnings/Questions

A place to collect what we learned and which questions we need to address after KB 2026.


\
* How can we best organize and plan trainings? Which locations can be booked early and at low rates?
* Are the shift lengths working? Do we need less people at certain times and more at other times?
* How do we maintain quality in our care work? Some are not able to attend trainings and we don't want to send Care Bears into situations they are not equipped to handle
* Do the structures we have (hut, tents) suffice or do we need different sized structures?
