## Description

Witch, please! Emerged from the idea of forest fairies, bitches and witches, bros and hoes and everything in between. We realized, we would not have survived 1419 and would have probably been burned. But it would have been worth it. We dance, we include, we make music, we will clean out your camps with our broomsticks to give something back. We will invite over for love potions, seances for all the people who ghosted us, for a cult creating session and a proper witch trial (no-one will burn, have no fear). We want to offer more, hence we reach out for help via Dreams. We will build a witch hut. We have the tent and the infrastructure to hold the hut up. We just need decor and some more hulaballoo. For this, we apply for art grants. Anything is helpful and will lighten up the place.

## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

We will be in the West Strip, very left corner of the Inn between, right now called "the witchy camp". The witch hut and dance floor will be the center of the Witchy Camp. It will be open for visitors. Here, we will conduct our witch trial, our seances for the people who followed us and here, the music will play. And, well. Witchy stuff will happen here.
