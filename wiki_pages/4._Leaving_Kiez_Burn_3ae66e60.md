## Leave no trace

As an LNT lead, you will need the following checklist to help do your best to keep us all environmentally on track!

### WHAT DO I DO?

- [ ] Organise or support people in your Kiez to carpool and plan their travel for low-carbon emissions
- [ ] Organize a garbage system and proper disposal of it.
- [ ] Plan communal soap, toiletries, etc as much as you are comfortable with.
- [ ] Plan and get people to do a camp sweep twice a day. Collect any MOOP. Check for glass shards. Keep the space clean & safe!
- [ ] Familiarise yourself with the carbon tracking spreadsheet and fill it out at the end of Kiezburn
- [ ] Plan and Stay for STRIKE, be prepared to take your camp´s stuff home. You shall be the last person standing on your Kiez ground.
- [ ] Bring an empty jar with a lid and a small hole in it for cigarette butts so they don't become scattered.

### STRIKE CHECKLIST:

- [ ] Remove all parts of any installation and building materials
- [ ] Remove props, costumes, and decorations
- [ ] Cables clean up
- [ ] Fill in any trenches that were dug for cables or gray water disposal
- [ ] Generator and petrol
- [ ] All rubbish and recycling
- [ ] Food waste has been taken to compost
- [ ] Gray water tipped out at the edge of the festival Gelände, try not to tip it all out in one place
- [ ] Nails, screws, tent pegs, and other small sharp items
- [ ] Cigarette butts
- [ ] All borrowed tools or materials returned
- [ ] Our Kiez are all accounted for and have got safe ways of getting home #soberdriver
