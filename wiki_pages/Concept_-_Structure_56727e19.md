# Concept - Structure

Welfare is manily focused on harm reduction and care, *not* medical, *not* security, and *not* therapy but overlaps with all three.

## **Core mission:**

* Emotional support
* Psychological first aid
* Safe space holding
* De-escalation
* Consent & interpersonal conflict support

## **Boundaries:**

* 🚫 No clinical diagnosis
* 🚫 No forced interventions (unless safety risk)
* 🚫 No acting as law enforcement

## Responsible divisions:

Site lead / Rangers = authority\nFirst aid / Medics = clinical\nWelfare = support\nRAD = support after event

## 

## **Team Structure**

### 🧠 Welfare Realizer Team 

* Owns vision, protocols, escalation decisions
* Coordinates with:
  * Rangers (Conflict resolution, Safety enforcement)
  * Medics (Physical issues, Severe intoxication)
  * Site leads (Serious incidents, Pattern recognition (repeat issues)

### 🌀 Shift Leads

* One per shift (day/night)
* Responsible for:
  * Team coordination
  * Decision-making on the ground
  * Escalations

### 🤝 Welfare Volunteers: Care Bears

* Core team
* Trained in:
  * Active listening
  * Grounding techniques
  * Crisis awareness
