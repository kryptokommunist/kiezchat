# Budget 2026

The total budget can be found in the google sheet below:

Notable changes compared to last year:

* Burnhalla gave us no profits, but Burn nights at birgit & bier did. YoY thats 20k€ were having less in 2026
* Were buying a second container for the site, as the old one is crazy full (+3k€)
* Were having to raise severals departments budgets by a bit each - toilets, lights, volunteer kitchen, rangers, all have small raises in budget
* Were introducing a safety buffer for unexpected expenses (+5k€)
* For the first time, kiezburn has an official budget for small satellite events like "Burners at a Bar" or similar, and for specific trainings for very involved burners with positions that *need* to go right, like RAD, site lead, finance lead, boardies etc.

In total that means were raising the ticket prices from 120€ in 2025 to 135€ in 2026
