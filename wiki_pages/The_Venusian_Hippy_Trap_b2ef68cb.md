## Description

"Somewhere in the forest a visitor from another world has landed… Reports suggest it looks like a place for wandering Hippies to rest. It's purpose still remains unknown… At night it radiates an otherworldly light.  But adventurers be warned: not everyone returns the same after a visit with The Venusian…"

*Extract from the Burner Inter-Dimensional Botanical Compendium (2026)*\n\nThe Venusian Hippy Trap is an interactive, atmospheric laser installation inspired by the Venus Fly Trap… It's designed to be a cosy night-time place to hang out, with interactive lighting… it is also a daytime rest-spot with shade from the sun.

It consists of several passive lights as a lure, with a mechanism to activate and interact with the lighting. The lighting sequence ends after some minutes and goes back to a neutral state. During the sequence there will be space for participants to leave without disruption.

__Fog and lasers included__.

## Safety and Accessibility 

**Safety:**\nThe Lasercube is fully compliant with the ILCA and to avoid any risk of prolonged visual contact it will never have a static beam. All faze effects will be done using an extra low output or netting.\n\nThe structure should be extremely stable due to its pyramid shape and support beams at the top and bottom. The legs and beams will be secured into the ground using rebar. \n\nA**ccessibility**: \nThere is a small 10cm step to get onto the platform, and a small back bend to get under the cover.
