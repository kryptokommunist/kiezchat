## Description

this is something id like to include for Greeters. Its gonna be awooden door frame (on wheels). Alternatively i can also see it being curtain, like in a theater, that we could dramatically open. 

Purpose of this is to make burners physically enter into the space to make the transition from default to burn. It might involve doorbells or other technical elements and props for greeters and burners to play with. After the Greetings are done (Last Shift is on Thursday) the door can be moved onto to lane and left as a playful structure.

## Environmental Impact

I will be using untreated wood to build the structure and doors that i can find for free. (looking good on ebay kleinanzeigen) Transport might be possible with help of volunteers and perhaps i can squeeze it into my camps van. The wood, since its big long beams, i would like to keep and return to my basement. If this turns out to be fun, i might bring it back bigger and better the year after. 


Part of the experience is turning the entry way into a soft introductions to the principles and add playful elements in it. White Board to write perhaps, little box of random (underneath the gifting principle) it is not fully fleshed out yet.
