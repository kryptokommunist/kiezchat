# Realizer communication

### **Goal**

Create **one volunteer checklist per area/realizer that has volunteer shifts**.

### Process

We divided outreach and follow-ups to communicates with the areas assigned to him


1. Contacting the assigned area/realizer.
2. Requesting **one finalized volunteer checklist** for that area.
3. Confirming the checklist with the team including all **volunteer shifts and key tasks**.
