## Description

The Fairy Teahouse invites you to take a rest in an enchanted corner of the forest. Here you can relax with a cup of tea and recharge with some yoga, a massage or cuddles in a cozy corner.

The Fairy Teahouse offers a kettle to brew some magical teas. The cups are part of an interactive light installation. The placement of the cups on a fairy table controls the light in the space and visitors can play with the different combinations it offers.

The floor is padded for extra comfort and there are seating cushions and a cozy cuddle corner with pillows and blankets as well as warm water bottles for cold nights.

## \nWhere at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

The Fairy Teahouse is placed in a tent with 5m diameter, and a remote corner in the forest. It could be in the forest on the far away corner of the runway, in the winding paths of the grove next to the runway (where the wizard hat tower is placed), or ina quiet corner in the forest.

It should be placed in a quiet area of the site, since its meant to be a calm and cozy place.

## Environmental Impact

The environmental impact comes mainly from the tent staying on the ground. The decoration and interior will be sourced from own material and fabrics, lights, furniture etc. that can be reused the next year.

The cooking of water for the tea will need the most energy. Otherwise the LED lighting and maybe a small speaker for ambient sound will be the only other electric elements of the installation.

The teacups will have to be cleaned regularly, which will need some water. The tea itself will be made with reusable sieves and is completely bio-degradeable.

## Safety and Accessibility 

It's a tent with decoration and fabrics in it. The main risk comes from the electrical wires, which will not be close to the fabrics.

Another safety hazard can be the hot water from the kettle. The placement will be stable and not easily reachable for little kids.

The tent's entrance can be a bit tricky to navigate with a wheelchair or walking impairments. With some help it should be possible to still enter the space.
