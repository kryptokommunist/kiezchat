## Description

The Chrono Stage will be at Cafe Wonderlands dance floor, where you can flow with the sound and dance into the experience of being just there in the here and now. The design itself is meant to question the concept of time, dissolving the concept of linear The big clock will hold several smaller clocks, all showing their own interpretation for time.

Depending on the funding budget the stage design can be enhanced by following enhancements:

* hypnoticaly rotating spiral of NOW
* LED-lit NOW
* A central clock with crazy moving hands
* LED ring around the big clock

## Existing funds

The tools used for assembling the stage will be provided by the builders. Also the DJ equipment, cables, plugs for power supply will be provided. Also, some parts of the structure might be built from material already in storage (e.g. table and chair)

## Safety and Accessibility 

The stage will be built in a stable way so it doesn't collapse on the people around it. It's mainly plywood and some light beams for stabilizing it.

It will be accessible to everyone who finds their way to  Cafe Wonderland.

## Experience

Our team has, designers, engineers and woodworkers with several years of experience building different structures and electronic devices (beds/furniture, interactive video mapping, LED-Dragon-Staff, …).
