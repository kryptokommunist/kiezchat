# 🚿 Shower of Power

## Description

The Shower of power at Saunacious Spa is the second iteration of last year's Shower Tower – the only warm shower on site at Kiezburn. The Shower of Power's shower tower is a wooden construction built on a wooden deck, featuring three showerheads and a propane-powered heating element.

At last year's Kiez Burn the showers were in constant use, ultimately resulting in the ground being over saturated with water. Therefore, in order to provide this amazing service once again this year, we have to construct a drainage system, collecting the grey-water under the showers and pumping it to the manhole. The manholes position is approximately 80m from the shower, with an elevation gain of approximately 3m. 

With the funds from this dream we want to construct and build a drainage system that can be used at Kiezburn's Saunacious Spa for many years to come.

## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

The Shower Tower will be located in the **Saunacious Spa** area, right next to the saunas.

## **#Environmental Impact**

The shower-tower itself was built last year and will be reused this year. We are constructing the drainage system from high-quality materials in order for it to be used over the next years. We are also providing eco-friendly soap in order to reduce environmental impact. One thing we cannot get around at the moment is using propane to heat the water – we have researched options for electric water heaters but the ones that deliver enough power to warm up enough water to shower properly require "starkstrom", which comes with a whole different set of problems.
