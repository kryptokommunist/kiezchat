## Description

It's like a small market stand but with a giant painted fish with a fake hook in its mouth on top. The cart will then have several taiyaki pans set up instead of any sort of griddle or waffle iron, but hanging. It will have lights going up the legs and under the fish for nighttime enjoyment as well as two lanterns. Under the top header will be little marine themed toys if you look into it. On occasion we will put the stand on wheels and take it somewhere popular to serve pancakes at night! Art by day, a bite a night (not applying for the pieces that are involved in serving as those are consumable). So it's kinda a cute fish cart that will be able to be laid on (the surface can take 250 kg) and you can look at the cute marine animals. It's like a surprise for short folks or people who investigate. It should be a cute photo opportunity and even cuter all lit up at night. 


## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

## Safety and Accessibility 

The base can hold 250 kg, so we will leave the counter open for people to sit/lay on it. Anything that is taller than shoulder height will be lightweight such that if it falls it will not hurt anyone.
