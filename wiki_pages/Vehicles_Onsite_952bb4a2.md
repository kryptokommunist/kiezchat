# 🛑GENERAL DRIVING RULES🛑


* **Only vehicles with a permit are allowed onsite during the event. To get the permit you need to apply within the deadline. Print out your permit, place the permit visibly behind your windshield and check in at the gate.**
* **EVERY vehicle moving onsite during the event needs an ESCORT (any sober person with a high visibility vest walking in front of the vehicle at all times it's moving)**
* **Please drive carefully and responsibly. People on foot have priority**
* **Emergency roads need to be kept clear at all times. No parking!** 
* **Think about others by not honking or idling**
* **You are allowed to move your vehicle only during the given time windows (Except for emergencies, production vehicles, and art cars)**


# ==🚜**BUILD AND STRIKE**==

During build and strike you can transport/move vehicles freely. If you park your vehicle onsite during build or strike it is best practise to leave your name and phone number and where to find you to the wind screen so people can reach you in case the vehicle needs to be moved.

## **When the event begins**  

Driving only with a permit. **Any vehicle without a permit needs to be driven offsite before this.**

## **When the strike begins**  

After this driving is free again.

### 🌳 Kiezes and their camper vehicles in the forest

The camper vehicles parked in the forest during the event will need 2 Black Safety Mats (Sicherheitsmatten) each under their motor for fire safety and environmental reasons. There is a limited amount available and the kiezes need to organise the pickup for their kiezes needs during the build. Since the forest is dense with trees and narrow pathways, camps must ensure in advance that their vehicles have sufficient space to maneuver when arriving / leaving - this is the responsibility of each Kiez. Please keep the emergency routes free at all times.
