# 👯‍♀️ Consent, Conflict and Awareness in camps

The Kiez Burn community strives to create inclusive spaces where everyone feels welcome, free, and safe to experiment and express themselves. In any large community, instances of conflict and harm between participants can occur, and our community is no exception. The Radical Accountability Division has collected some resources that can be useful in case of a conflict or incidents of consent violation or other harm. This information is essential for a camp lead. You might also choose to have a Consent Lead in your camp who is responsible for these topics.


**The Code of Conduct**

Make sure you as a camp lead have read the Code of Conduct! This is the outline on how we are expected to treat each other. In case you have a camp member whose behaviour is problematic you can always refer them to the Code of Conduct.

<https://docs.google.com/document/d/1nGCRtXA6y8ggkwQ8FuQqRc1_XjJnwPXjGtBYDRlp8pc/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.mwn4yjz9sfoo>


**How to make Kiez Burn Safer Together**

A short guide on how anyone can help make our community safer by having clear boundaries oneself, supporting others and interfering unwanted behaviour with low threshold. As a camp lead you can show example and be prepared in case you need to lead some more difficult conversations in your camp.

<https://docs.google.com/document/d/1d7Dauc7uE6Xx6AV-H0vQusxNkJwVyV5vy6iPYmQF41k/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.6xkwj1i08mc8>

**Relationship Survival Guide**

A couple having a relationship meltdown in the camp can be uncomfortable for everyone. You can encourage the couples in your camp to check out the  guide as a preparation for the burn.

**Support Resources for People Struggling**

A burn can sometimes be a triggering environment and it's not uncommon that someone's mental health problems escalate during the burn. Something traumatising can also happen. We have collected a list of free resources where people can find professional support in case of emotional distress, available even on a phone.

<https://docs.google.com/document/d/1dn4viGvgAyrWPCcp_vPz2On6SOfNwz__FChWQ9Jix0k/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.jncw4a148i2b>
