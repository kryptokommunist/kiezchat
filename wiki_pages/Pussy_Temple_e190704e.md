## Description

*The Pussy Temple* is an immersive dome space centering pussy power through care-based, embodied creative practice. Designed as a soft, layered environment, it offers workshops, rituals, cuddle parties, acoustic jam sessions and rest zones that foster kinesthetic listening, reciprocity, and radical connectedness. The space is conceived as a **"living organism"**: emergent, relational, and responsive.

We invite workshop facilitators (FLINTA-focused) who align with our vision of inclusive queer-feminism and collective care. This already includes movement labs, drawing sessions, and participatory rituals that explore art as transformation and social change. (more applications encouraged!)

The Pussy Temple functions as both sanctuary and cultural incubator: where pleasure, rest, and creativity are treated as essential, political acts contributing to a more equitable cultural landscape in our burner community by prioritizing underrepresented voices and reimagining how we gather, create, and care together.

## Power usage estimate

Lighting for 7 hours (one day)

* LED strips: 3 × 20W × 7h = 420Wh
* Sunset lamps: 3 × 10W × 7h = 210Wh

Music setup for 2 hours:

* JBL Party Speaker: 80W × 2h = 160Wh
* DJ controller: 10W × 2h = 20Wh
* Laptop: 60W × 2h = 120Wh

Total:

* 420 + 210 + 160 + 20 + 120 = 930Wh
* About 0.93 kWh total energy per day
