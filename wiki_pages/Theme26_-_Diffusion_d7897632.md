# Theme26 - Diffusion

A Burn probably doesn't need a theme to be an exceptionally creative event. Kiez Burn has had more or less inspiring themes in the past, but there seems to be a general agreement around the fact that it's been a couple of years that the theme has rather more silly than inspiring. 

It was one of these hangouts, after the general assembly, where we were playing with ideas for a theme and "diffusion" came up. Diffusion - because yes, KB26 will take place right during Fusion; a clash that we have been avoiding for years and couldn't prevent in 26. Beyond this calendar aspect, this appeared to be an inspiring theme. Then time passed, the Kickoff week-end & Burnhalla happened and most people hearing about this theme liked it, which led to decide not to submit the choice of a theme to a public votation. And to not even write an AP about it! 


*Diffusion offers a polysemic playground, to be understood and interpreted in various ways: scientifically, intellectually, poetically, politically or im/materially. From smells to ideas, from liquids to cultures, from good vibes to bad MOOPs and from cucumbers to Kiezettes, let's be more intentional about what we diffuse, and how we diffuse it.*
