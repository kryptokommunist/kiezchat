## Description

I want to bring a Parkour Scaffolding and two big round bales of straw to move around, play and chill on. \nI will put some hammocks on the scaffolding which can be removed for movement workshops but otherwise used for cozy chilling. Bales of straw are nice to sit/chill on, but also so fun to jump onto and over! 

I think it could be awesome for movement workshops like parkour, creative movement, pole dance, playing catch, etc as well as a playground for spontaneous movement shenanigans - and a cozy place to chill out otherwise :)


## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

I would like to have it on grass near the strip.\nIdeally on the south side, somewhere near-ish the ant, but I'm flexible.

## Environmental Impact

The Scaffolding will return to the Parkour club and will be fully re-used. \nMy idea for the straw would be to burn it at the Temple or Effigy Burn, I will connect with the relevant people if the dream is accepted. Update: I found someone who will pick them up during strike for free, so they will be used afterwards as well, nothing going to waste :)

## Cost Estimate

The main cost of the bales of straw is them being transported to the venue, I've communicated with a seller on ebay about the price.\nThe transport of the scaffolding will happen alongside other materials for Kiezburn. The poles are long, but in the other dimensions don't take up that much space so I've calculated 1/10th of the rent + fuel of one transporter from Berlin (two days with two trips each so the transporter can go back to Berlin). \nOne trip with a Miles transporter is about 125€, so overall it'd be 125\*4/10=50€. I hope to be able to do it with an own transporter, but I want to plan for the case where that doesn't work out.

## Safety and Accessibility 

The movement part of the dream is accessible for all those who can and want to move! For those who don't, the dream is also meant for chilling in hammocks and on the straw bales.\nThe structure stands on grass, which prevents fall injuries. I will put up signs to discourage intoxicated hippies from climbing.
