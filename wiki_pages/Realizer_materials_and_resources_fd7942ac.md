## **Team Structure**

### 🧠 Welfare Lead (You or appointed)

* Owns vision, protocols, escalation decisions
* Coordinates with:
  * Rangers
  * Medics
  * Org leads

### 🌀 Shift Leads

* One per shift (day/night)
* Responsible for:
  * Team coordination
  * Decision-making on the ground
  * Escalations

### 🤝 Welfare Volunteers

* Core team
* Trained in:
  * Active listening
  * Grounding techniques
  * Crisis awareness

### 🌙 Sanctuary / Space Holders (optional but powerful)

* Hold physical space (chill tent, decompression area)
* Focus on vibe, not problem-solving


---

## 3. **Functional Areas**

### 🏕️ Welfare Space (Sanctuary)

* Quiet, low-stimulation zone
* Always staffed
* Key features:
  * Shade / warmth
  * Water, electrolytes
  * Blankets, mats
  * Soft lighting at night


---

### 🚶 Outreach / Roaming Team

* Walk the event
* Identify:
  * Overwhelmed participants
  * Conflict situations
  * People on difficult journeys


---

### 🧯 Incident Response

Triggered when:

* Someone is in distress
* Consent violation reported
* Emotional breakdown / panic / dissociation

Flow:


1. Assess safety
2. Stabilize
3. Decide: stay / escalate / handover
