## SAFETY 

Our fire safety concept includes fire safety shifts to watch out for the fire spinners. We have fire blankets, fire extinguishers, and first-aid products on hand, and we are in contact with the Kiez Burn fire safety realizer. The fuel we use for burning is more expensive but burns cleaner and is gentler on the lungs of spinners, those taking a fire safety shift, and the audience. We also offer FFP2 masks for the people on fire safety shift. 

To join the fire jams, we expect every player to be educated in fire safety, safe dipping, and of course, to know their tools well enough. We offer a fire safety workshop before the opening of the fire space, which all people that take fire safety shifts are expected to join.\n

## Art Statement 

The Fire Space serves as a stage for performers to manipulate fire props such as poi, staffs, or hoops. This manipulation of fire creates visually striking patterns and movements - a dance with the flames.

This immersive artistic experience transforms the physical environment into a canvas for creative expression, blending elements of movement, light, and sound to evoke emotional and aesthetic responses from both participants and observers.

## Environmental Impact and LNT

The fire jams will burn synthetic fuel. We are trying to minimize the environmental impact by using only synthetic high-quality fuel for cleaner burning. This choice also makes up for the majority of the cost for this Dream.

To prevent the spilling of synthetic oil into the ground, the area under the dipping station will be covered with an oil-proof tarp. We chose a location on asphalt to reduce the impact of potential oil drops on the surrounding nature.
