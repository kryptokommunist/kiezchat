## Description

We want to bring some normal vibes to Kiezburn. we will build a small boring , middle-class single-family home. It'll be a place where people can get together for everyday activities, mow the lawn, and chat over the garden fence, get invited for a sausage or a beer. And of course some nasty suprises.

We will be there a couple of hours a day beeing "normal" and interacting with people. The aim is to make a contrast. Something you wouldnt expect; the camp might be evolving over time.

Inside, there will be a kids' room, a spot for homework, and probably boring Family dinners at 7. So whenever its getting too hectic you can retreat here and pretend that there is nothing extraodinary happenig.
