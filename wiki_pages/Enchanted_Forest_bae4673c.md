# 🧚 Enchanted Forest

Deep in the heart of Secret Forest, beyond the glowing lanterns and crooked mushrooms, lies a strange and magical place where whispers echo through the trees and the forest floor hums with curious energy. The Enchanted Forest is a dreamy, immersive experience inviting all wandering creatures to step into a world of mystery, wonder, and delightful nonsense.


A place where curiosity guides the way and logic has been politely asked to leave its shoes at the entrance.

## Description

This year, Café Wonderland goes Enchanted Forest — transforming our beloved camp into a mystical woodland dreamscape full of hidden corners, magical lights, cozy nests, and curious surprises.


By day, the forest becomes a playful sanctuary for gathering, exploring, and slowing down. Visitors can wander through charmingly decorated spaces filled with fabrics, plants, glowing objects, strange little details, and comfy chill areas. Expect workshops, storytelling, spontaneous performances, tea-fueled conversations, silly happenings, and soft world music drifting through the trees like a sleepy spell.


As the sun disappears, the forest awakens in new ways. Twinkling lights and glowing installations guide guests deeper into the woods, where a magical stage hosts karaoke chaos, downtempo journeys, housy grooves, live performances, and DJs weaving together worldly nighttime magic. Somewhere between a dream and a feverish fairy tale, a silly little bar serves nighttime tea, and perhaps stranger potions for those lucky enough to stumble upon them.


The Enchanted Forest is not a spectacle to observe, but a world to step into. A communal playground for imagination, interaction, and joyful absurdity.


## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

The Enchanted Forest will be located within the grounds of Café Wonderland, nestled into the surrounding Secret Forest, creating an immersive and magical extension of the natural environment.

## Environmental Impact and LNT

Our forest magic is built with a strong focus on sustainability, reuse, and Leave No Trace principles. Much of our décor, fabrics, furniture, lighting, and infrastructure has been lovingly reused and maintained from previous years of Café Wonderland.

We prioritize:

* reclaimed and second-hand materials
* reusable decorations and structures
* LED lighting for low power consumption
* careful waste separation and cleanup
* minimal single-use materials

Our build and strike are organized with dedicated Leave No Trace awareness to ensure the forest remains exactly as magical after we leave as before we arrived.

## Existing funds

Café Wonderland has always been a completely public and welcoming camp, lovingly built up over the past 7 years. Thanks to ongoing community support, past fundraisers, and shared passion, we've been able to accumulate and maintain a wide range of equipment that forms the foundation of our space each year:

* tent structures & weatherproof coverings
* couches, carpets, cozy furniture
* large collection of art and decorations
* lighting, electric cables

This growing inventory allows us to build sustainably, reuse wherever possible, and focus our resources on replacing worn-out essentials and expanding creative elements. It's a shared treasure chest of magic — built by many, for all. 

Over the years, Café Wonderland has collected a delightful trove of decorations, costumes, and peculiar props, most of which will be reused. However, as with all magical things, some items have vanished into the matrix or crumbled into glittery dust with time. 

While we do our best to repair and upcycle, funding support would help us replace essential infrastructure and keep the wonder alive.


## Safety and Accessibility 

Creating a welcoming and safe environment is an important part of Café Wonderland. Pathways and communal areas will be clearly lit and organized to allow easy movement through the space, especially during nighttime hours.


Weatherproof tents, tarps, and sheltered areas help protect guests, furniture, and infrastructure from changing weather conditions. Electrical installations and cables will be carefully secured and monitored to minimize risks.


The space is designed to remain open, inclusive, and inviting to all curious creatures who wish to wander through the forest.


## Experience

The Café Wonderland crew consists of experienced burners, artists, builders, musicians, performers, and organizers who have been co-creating immersive spaces at KiezBurn and other burns for many years.

Our team has extensive experience in:

* camp infrastructure and build logistics
* stage and sound hosting
* interactive art and decoration
* workshops and performance curation
* sustainable camp organization and Leave No Trace practices

We are a passionate and joyful community excited to once again bring a colorful, magical corner to KiezBurn.
