# Harm reduction - setup, inventory, planning

The idea is to offer an approachable inviting space near the Welfare hut. 

Inside the tent, there will be desks and shelves where information and harm reduction materials are available.

This is a consumption free area.


### Offered materials

* some books and leaflets are provided for reading in place but need to stay with harm reduction
* flyers and materials to take away for information or consumption and use
* test kits for substances (not clear yet, whether they can be used in the space or rather not - question for moop situation)


### Inventory

* Harm Reduction banner
* Banner about substance combinations
* Books and information materials
* Remaining tests and materials from 2025
