# Placement 2026

This year, placement is a coordinated and facilitated process!



1. The first round of placement applications happens before Spark
2. Before Spark, camps and art that applied for placement will be sent a placement suggestion by the placement team, and will get a chance to accept or request changes during Spark or via email.
3.  A preliminary map is published after Spark for the wider community
4. The final deadline is June 1st. After that, the final map will be published by the placement team.


We will do our best to accomodate everyone's needs while respecting the sound protocol, facilitating the flow and enabling the best experience for everybody. Please be patient and trust the process!
