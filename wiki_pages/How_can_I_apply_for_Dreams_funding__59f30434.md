# How can I apply for Dreams funding?

NOW YOU CAN'T DEADLINE HAS PASSED

**Here is your checklist:**


1. Familiarize yourself with the funding rules [here ](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/dreams-funding-rules-pgVOvKrQ3s)(yes, again, in case you missed it): 
2. Make sure you are signed in here with you Kiezburn account [(Link)](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/dreams-and-art-sbOf7Kno16)
3. Duplicate the wiki Page "[Dream Template](/doc/dream-template-gSI6Lo4jLs)"(this is a link) and fill in all the sections and Publish it. Or start a New Doc on the left side on Dreams application folder "+" 'name it' and then use a "Template Latest" from the top right button with shapes. (Deadline is **22 of May Midnight**) 
4. Fill out the form at the bottom.
5. Click the "…" to subscribe to your dream: this will be our first channel of communication with you. If nobody has contacted you after two weeks, please contact us on Discord here <https://discord.gg/MvG7XvPg>

**The next steps after that are:**


5. The dream guides will come together in a dream team meeting and decide if your dream will get funded by Kiezburn or not
6. Your dream guide will tell you about the decision of the council:

   
   1. If it was a No: your project's story ends here. Sorry, we can't fund all your amazing ideas! (for appeals or other comments, see the bottom of [this article](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/dreams-funding-rules-pgVOvKrQ3s#h-appeals)) 

      
      1.  And if was a "no, but,"  your dream guide will talk to you about what to improve and how to resubmit your application
   2. If it was a Yes:  follow the next steps.
7. It's time for you to start building your project, and bring it to the burn! Please remember to keep your receipts and put Kiezburn e.V. on all your bills ESPECIALLY online orders! or else no refund. [Details can be found here.](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/refunds-guidelines-RV26wf4uyK)
