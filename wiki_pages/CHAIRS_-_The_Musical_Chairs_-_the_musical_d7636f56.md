## Environmental Impact

We will organize Chairaoke for the audience at the end of the show. Since we do not have a television or a large screen that we could bring, we will print the lyrics on actual paper (100 times) and have a chair origami in the back so that people can reuse the paper. We are planning to organize pop-up Chairaoke at other moments of the Burn, so the lyrics can be reused. We'll collect all the ones that will not be needed. Props and chairs will be reused for future Burn events and chair-related performances.

## Safety and Accessibility 

Safety: Viewers of the musical might start to love sitting too much so that they forget to stand up. We cannot be made responsible for any form of back pain from prolonged sitting. Remember: Sit and ***stand***, sit and ***stand***, chair, chair, chair, chair, chair, chair, chair, chair!

Accessibility: It might be that not all of the jokes will be accessible to people unaware of the greatness of chairs. Other than that we rely on Next Stage's accessibility concept, but it should be possible to watch using your own (wheel)chair.
