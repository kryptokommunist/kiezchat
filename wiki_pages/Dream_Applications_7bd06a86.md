# Dream Applications

Here is where all Dream Applications live and breath.\nShare the link of it to people you are collaborating with.

Please **click on** the three dots next to [Dream Template](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/dream-template-27-mar-lHVSdNAuBe) 1 in the **left menu bar** and select "**duplicate"** to start filling it in! 


==AFTER== DUPLICATION start by renaming the title of the duplicate to your Project Name and emoji if want(you click on it to change it)! 

In spirit of transparency and hopes of giving everyone a better understanding how Dream funding works the dream guides will ask questions in the comments section of your page. You can subscribe to comments if you press on three dots at the top right of *your* project page, but that's not obligatory and we will reach out to you personally using the contacts you provide in the secret data protected part if there's no response.
