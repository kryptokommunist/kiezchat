## Description

Enter a dreamy children's paradise with a mighty Bouncy Castle at the burn for the little ones (age up until 11)! Next to a giant "connect 4" for all the ages. We will bring other smaller outdoor structures to entertain the little ones, such as a paddle pool. We envision this space to be used every day during the daytime for a couple of hours, pending suitable weather. 


:::info
We could also "upgrade" the bouncy castle to an adult version to make it more inclusive for everyone aged 11+, but it would need to be from a different provider (at a higher cost, likely incl. transport by the provider due to volume & weight). It would also require us to do thorough searches of the bouncy castle throughout use in case anything fell out of the pockets **💊.**

:::

## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

*In which kiez, or on the art plateau? How big or small do you imagine it?*

Ideally in close proximity to the OnlyFams camp. It will take aprox. 6x6 m of floor space. Please note that for safety we'll need to secure the Bouncy Castle in the ground (on even grass). However, it is possible to also rent [heavy sacks](https://www.hüpfburg.de/wassersack-huepfburgen-mieten.html) at an extra cost (plus more material to transport it) which allows you to have the bouncy castle on the tarmac directly, should space be an issue.

## Safety and Accessibility 

*how is this structure*

For safety reasons there will always need to be one adult supervising when the bouncy castle is in use. There is also the weather factor, since it needs to be deflated when wind speed is <38 km/h. It can be up even with mild rain, although we would make those decisions on the spot 

## Experience

*If you have previous experience, write it down here so that we can get a better picture about you and what you can do. Even if it's not exactly related to this project, If you find some connection we'd like to hear about them.*
