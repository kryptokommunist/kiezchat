# Kiez Burn 2026 Programme & Stage Schedule

## Overview

The official programme is on the **Dust app** at https://dust.events/ — use it to browse all events, filter by theme or location, star favorites, and print a personal schedule.

Events can be added to Dust until June 22nd. Events submitted before June 8th appear in the printed programme.

## Stages & Spaces

### KiezPlatz (main central stage)
The large community stage in central playa. Open for workshops, talks, dance, movement, performances — anything! Open from 9am to 9pm.
- Speaker system available (JBL Charge 6), mic included
- **Sign up to host an event:** https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Tweb4vTxkDyoMx--VfYXBR2Rlms80FsAX1F4QKjuTcs/edit?usp=sharing
- To appear in the printed programme, submit by June 7th
- No application process — just write your event on the sheet or directly on the on-site blackboard

### BurnHalla
The large indoor venue / bar space.
- **Sign up to perform or bring an event:** https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfA7FkO0z3me0oBPFL_XrfrrX0dSsDEWODqrqs1ZA9CQXKOVg/viewform?usp=publish-editor
- **DJ/music sign-up:** https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSc-RZZcZ1tl2tMWLZWXeI8HWaEJp6c9QDj4I0FKQJUrvZ24uA/viewform

### The Next Stage
Talks, quizzes, karaoke, performances, cabaret, concerts.
- **Sign up to perform:** https://yh.lucmartinon.com/nextcloud/apps/forms/s/StzK4Js9xE488kHXftLXswwq

### The Chrono Stage
At Cafe Wonderland's dance floor. Music and movement-focused.

### Solardome
Solar-powered dome stage for music and gatherings.

### Fire Space (Promethean Fire Space)
Opens in the evenings. Fire performances.
