# Kiez Burn Official Website (mmm.kiezburn.org)

## Event Overview

Kiez Burn is a non-profit cultural event that celebrates beauty and life in accordance with 11 Principles. It takes place **23–29 June 2026 near Berlin**. The 2026 theme is "diffusion."

Everyone is welcome to join. The motto: **"Participate, don't spectate."**

Founded in 2017 as a Berlin-based adaptation of Burning Man principles. Registered as Kiez Burn e.V. (Amtsgericht Berlin-Charlottenburg, VR37426B) in 2019. Weserstr. 13A, 10247 Berlin.

Previous editions: 2024 event was June 19–23 with 1,250 participants. First event near Cottbus attracted 500 attendees.

There are no cash transactions at the main venue — it is a cashless, gift-based economy.

---

## The 11 Principles

Our principles make it so we all have a common guideline in building our community. They are the pillars of our common experience during Kiez Burn.

1. **Participation** — Participate, don't spectate. Kiez Burn happens only when you get involved. The community emphasizes radical participation and transformative change through personal involvement.

2. **Radical Self-Expression** — Individual expression comes from unique gifts. It is offered as a gift to others with respect for recipients' rights.

3. **Radical Self-Reliance** — You are responsible for your physical and emotional wellbeing. People should rely on inner resources and ask for help when needed.

4. **No Commerce** — There is nothing to buy or sell here folks! Commerce is removed to foster cooperation without money.

5. **Leave No Trace** — Community members care for the environment and take responsibility for removing everything they bring.

6. **Community** — A family of individuals, we look after each other through cooperation, collaboration, and unity.

7. **Inclusion** — We welcome everyone for their unique contribution to our community, with respect and tolerance.

8. **Gifting** — Gifts come from the heart without expectation of return or equal exchange.

9. **Communal Effort** — Focus on creative cooperation supporting social networks and public spaces.

10. **Immediacy** — Make now count. Experience the present moment fully.

11. **Consent** — We believe only YES means YES, with emphasis on clear verbal communication and boundary respect.

## Tickets

Tickets can be obtained through two pathways:

- **First-Come-First-Served Sale** — Visit tickets.kiezburn.org to purchase through standard channels.
- **Directed Ticket System** — A referral system where the ticketing team distributes initial invitations. After purchasing, recipients may receive additional invitations to pass on to others.

Ticket sales for 2026 began March 29. Registration deadline: June 1.
