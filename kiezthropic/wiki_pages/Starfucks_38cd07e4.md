## Description

It's been a turbulent year at Starfucks. Global oil prices have gone up, coffee transport to our HQ has been delayed and as a consequence there have been massive layoffs among our staff. Which we regret. But also celebrate: Our stock price has actually risen. We are looking forward to a successful and more profitable year 2026, with our highlight being the participation at Kiezburn. We will bring a hardcore crew, fantastic coffee and a sleek appearance to the burn. Our goal remains the same: Increase stakeholder value and create maximum profit, all while working on providing a unique coffee experience for our customers. 

## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

We will appear in a smaller form at Kiezburn this year, and would love to be placed at the strip, next to camps like Just Jessie. We want to make it as easy as possible for customers to recognise the nearest Starfucks location.

## Existing funds

We have had Starfucks for multiple years now and have financed the basic structure through membership fees at Kiezburn and other burns. That is a tent (approx. 600€), a high-end coffee machine (approx. 2500€) and wooden furniture (recycled). All we need for this year is the transport to keep Starfucks running, after we had to let go multiple staff and are a bit stripped down this year.

## Safety and Accessibility 

We have a basic, sturdy tent that can be attached to the ground and resists strong winds. It's worked very well over the years.

## Experience

Starfucks Inc. is a corporate coffee chain, bringing coffee delicacies to Burns around Europe. We are a trusted source of black gold and wherever Starfucks Inc. has risen their tent, customers joyfully screamed at the prospect of having a high-end coffee experience. We've become an institution and have plenty of experience in providing the kickstarter of the day to lovely humans. That being said: Don't worry about our experience, worry about being the first in line to get a delicious coffee each morning!

## Power usage estimate

We will need one full outlet, because the coffee machine's voltage requires it. We could share the outlet with camps like Just Jessie, because that camp only requires basic electricity for lights and phone charging. But we cannot share it with a camp that requires high voltage output, not even for a water boiler etc.
