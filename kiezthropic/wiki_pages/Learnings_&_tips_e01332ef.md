## **Adding camp members**

* Discuss the process of accepting new members __before__ (e.g. how many camp members do you want to have, what are the acceptance criteria, is there a possibility for vetoes?)
* Find out how many "sparklings" (new burners) you are comfortable with to "train/take care of" – a ratio of maximum 1/3 sparklings + 2/3 experienced (and maybe also known) people in the camp makes integration easier. 
* Especially for "sparklings" (new burners) it's good to have a more experienced buddy/mentor and a backup.

## Camp lead role

* What are responsibilities, boundaries and expectations (!) of the lead (e.g. communication, conflict resolution, escalation point?)
* Many camps find daily check-in rounds during the Burn helpful. This can happen during meals.

## Workload and tasks

* Is everyone aware of the fact that this is a participatory event? Discuss shifts, workload distribution, mutual support, "burnout" prevention. In what capacity can everyone be involved? Before the event, during, after? What involvement is necessary?

  here you can find a document from a camp that collected all infos around their shift system with points 

  [https://docs.google.com/document/d/1mWi4px0Fn7QmgA3QY3PZgHw4NPOyC4maNUIk7ISLSOw/edit?usp=sharing](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1mWi4px0Fn7QmgA3QY3PZgHw4NPOyC4maNUIk7ISLSOw/edit?usp=sharing)

* Which key roles to consider in your camp? (e.g. leads for kitchen, deco/dream, build, strike, awareness/consent, transport/storage, LNT, power, shit ninja, program/workshops, finances, camp shower/water, possibly for shifts)
* How self reliant vs. co-dependent does your camp want to be? (e.g. food – everyone brings their own vs. every meal will be prepared together)

## Communication

* How can everyone be reached? (e.g. important infos by mail/on your own Discord server?)

## Burner culture and spirit

* The most important gift you can give to our community (and responsibility of every burner): Educate on our principles! On our diverse community! You're the biggest part in creating the burn you want to experience!
* Encourage everyone to introduce themselves with their pronouns.
