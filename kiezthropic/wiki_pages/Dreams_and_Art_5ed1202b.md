# 🎨 Dreams and Art

## Welcome Dreamers!

This is your first stop to getting your Dreams funded though our art grant that **Anyone** can apply for.

* We will stop taking applications earlier if we run out of money. (Last year we ran out of money)

###  If approved, here are some more Fast Facts:

* Keep your Receipts! Take a Clear photo of them when you get them! Do NOT lose them as we will need them from you. We work on a reimbursement basis, No receipt, No moneys. (There have been super duper special pre-funding situations, like unified wood purchase for many dreams, which we are happy to talk about.)
* Remember, we **CANNOT** split receipts. Example: You pay for gasoline for approved transport, but in the same receipt there is pack of gum, This makes it not payable. OR you only have gasoline in the receipts but wish to only get half of it paid back because you went over budget, This is also not possible.
* Add KiezBurn address to all invoices that have an address, Especially online ones!

  Kiez Burn e.V.\nc/o de Burca\nWeserstraße 13A\n10247 Berlin
* Read up on @[Refunds Guidelines](mention://e6212b69-e441-41a3-948c-03ddf8522c72/document/2116d2c4-bcb6-426d-9b1c-71a54ec7e5b1) which is more comprehensive and includes a template for private invoices/receipts.
* If you don't bring what was funded, you will not get money for whatever you already paid for.
* **==Deadline 2==** for receipts is **26th of July**, 1 month after the burn ended.\n

  ## What are Dreams?

  \
  Dreams are art pieces, performances, transformative spaces, but also infrastructure improvements and such for the community, that are funded by Kiezburn dream grants. Most projects are brought by participants without any compensation, but some burners need a little help. That's where the dream team comes in, they are helping dreamers with:


* Trying to keep you up-to-date with all info including with the bureaucracy part (depending on their availability, also help with it).
* feedback for increasing the chances of getting funded by KB dream grants
* connecting to other dreamers or Kiezburn realizers for building help, placement, power, or cheaper material sources
