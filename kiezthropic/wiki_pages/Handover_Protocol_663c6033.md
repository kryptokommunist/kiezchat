# Handover Protocol

The handover between shifts starts around 15 minutes to the full hour at the end of the shift. The Lead Care Bear who was on shift will moderate the round.

Feel free to adapt this however it works best for you.


**Gather all volunteers** from the former and the new shift (If Care Bears are involved in active ongoing cases they might need to stay on and have an individual handover later - check in if that is feasible for them)

**Greet everyone** and ask the round to check in with themselves how their body, mind and heart are feeling - maybe take a slow breath to arrive in the here and now

Now invite everyone to **introduce themselves** (name and pronouns) and to briefly **share how they are doing** after/before their shift (those who were on shift may also share if something happened and is still on their mind)

Share yourself how your are doing and also **share a brief overview** of ongoing cases and the situation at the safer space

The next parts can be done separately with the ending shift and the starting shift

* Ask the volunteers of the **ending shift** if they wish for support in leaving their role as Care Bear (like a short talk, a hug, some light body tapping,...) and if they can name something good that they are planning to do for themselves and share it with the group (meet friends, going to the sauna, sleeping, …)
* Ask the volunteers of the **starting shift** if they have desires for their shift (moving around or rather staying at info desk, a lot of fluffing, …) and that they share boundaries/fears (not wanting to deal with consent violations, rather not supporting in substance related cases, no vomit, no blood,...)
