## Description

Primarily this dream will be bringing a powerful laser printer to site, setting it up in a central (mostly dry) location, which could be the Ranger HQ or Toolhouse, and ensuring it is supplied with toner and paper and instructions of use.


Additionally we (the Kiezette team) will use this to print an issue or two on site. We can't commit to the 12 issues we made last year (without any LLM use at all!), but we still want to bring some news to the people. 


## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

Somewhere central and accesible to those in the know. It couldn't be located in such a way that everyone can simply print whatever they want (moop issues), but all camps would be communicated instructions of use so they can print things for their public offerings.
