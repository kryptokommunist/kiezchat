## Environmental Impact

The saunas and decor are recyclable and used with love. Admittedly our Saunas run on firewood and thereby set free CO2

For our fire barrels however we try to collect wood leftovers as much as possible. We even aim to make a pitch to all camps during/ after Build for them to drop their leftover wood at our camp. Hence, they don't have to take care of it and we will find a usage.

## Safety and Accessibility 

* We bring fire extinguishers: At least 1 per Sauna 
* Fire Safety protocol: We set up a fire safety protocol every year and are in close contact with the KB Fire 
* We always have two people from our camp on site who are sober. They are not only taking care of the Saunas but also facilitating the space. 
* The Saunas will be closed when not being supervised by our Camp

## Experience

This is the fifth year in the row we are bringing the Saunas to Kiez Burn. Beyond hosting Saunacious Spa, our Camp has been hosting Saunacious Fundraiser and many other events at 90°. This is a Sauna Space in Berlin which several of our camp members are actively involved in.
