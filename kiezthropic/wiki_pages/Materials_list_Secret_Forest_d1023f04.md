## Fire extinguishers

**Every Kitchen** needs to have a **6 kg ABC fire extringuisher** AND a sign pointing at its location. This is nessessary for the Ordnungsamt and fire security. This is also important for free campers. 

Kiez Burn rents enough fire extinguishers for 75 percent of camps from secret forest. If you want to rent one, you can get it at toolhouse from build friday on (19th of june) and have to return it on strike monday (29th of june). 

Toolhouse will put down your information (name, e-mail, phone number) and give this list to our Schatzmeister. 

You will have to pay a renting fee to Kiez Burn: \nKiez Burn e.V.\nDE11 4306 0967 1066 5874 00\nFire Extinguisher Rental \[campname\]\n36€
