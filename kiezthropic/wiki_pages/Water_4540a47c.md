# How to get connected:

you put your T-junction next to the main hose, this can be anywhere near a main hose, it does not need to be an endpoint "DO NOT CUT THE HOSE YOURSELF", its likely pressurised, now you either wait for a Sven to connect you, or find a Sven, alternatively you can reach me on a radio, this might or might not make your process quicker on being connected, 


see the production map: 

https://goo.gl/maps/tnyBqHCFbc81cfST7\n\nwater are the thick lines, look in the legenda on the left if water is enabled, and which are water lines, if you are more than 50 meters away form a waterline (which is the case for some camps), there is some spare to come to your direction\n\nthe thick lines are water, the thin ones are unrelated to water, if water is not showing, activate on the left side\n\nWhat to be careful with:

Garden hoses and other gardena quality stuff might burst under the high pressure, normal garden hoses often also are produced with lead inside, making them unsuitable for drinking water, i know many have gardena stuff in your camp, its just a warning that depending on the pressure this might not work,


another important thing to note, gardenhoses have a inner diameter of 13mm, the rest of the network is 25mm, this will support the longer distances we need to travel, if your neighbour uses 25mm PE, and you use gardenhose, you likely wont get mcuh water when they use their water (or in some cases you wont get anything at all due to pressure loss)
