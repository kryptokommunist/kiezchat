## ComfortZone´s UV paradise

We've already taken the first glowing step toward our **ComfortZone UV Paradise** dream at **BurnHalla** — the image above shows the floor we created there, and it gave us a real glimpse of what this idea can become. Now we want to bring the vision to its full potential with one final essential upgrade: **four large 120cm NEON UV tubes** (plus a few replacement tubes to keep the magic alive)

These are not the newer UV lamps that sadly tend to blind people and mostly work only as passive wall lighting. We are specifically looking for proper UV tubes that can be mounted safely and effectively on **walls and ceilings**, creating a full-room immersive glow instead of just lighting up one corner. 🌌

We already own **UV-reactive strings, UV tapes, and some Tüll** — the incredibly UV-reactive fabric visible on the left side of the image as well as a starter-kit of uv lamps. But the difference between some uv lights and a real **UV Paradise** is coverage. Those large 120cm tubes would allow us to spread the glow eeeevverrryyywherree. The result would be a reusable, flexible, and instantly transformative setup that can turn almost any space into a glowing ComfortZone dreamscape. 💫

This project is especially worth funding because it is **not a one-time decoration**. Everything is reusable and perfect for future co-ops, floors, pop-up events, and spontaneous shenanigans. Once we have the tubes, we can bring the UV Paradise to many different spaces again and again. We are already in talks with another floor that might want to be "UV'd up" as well — so the impact would go beyond just one space. 🖤💜

Funding this would help us create a unique visual identity for ComfortZone: playful, immersive, recognizable, a tad 80s. It would make our spaces feel more magical, more neon, more memorable — the kind of environment people walk into and instantly feel ✨💫🌈

With this upgrade, our UV Paradise can become a real, reusable piece of infrastructure for the community — something that keeps glowing from event to event.

No promises at this point, but we are dreaming about making this setup mobile — so we could bring little bursts of UV magic to unexpected corners of Kiezburn. ✨💜
