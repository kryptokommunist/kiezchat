# 5. Getting involved

## ✨ Get Involved – Participate in Creating Kiez Burn

The main way to get involved with Kiez Burn is through your Kiez' interactive offering, but the central org volunteer run and also needs a lot of people to function. We always need people to take volunteer shifts, lead roles before and during the event.

You might also be interested in how to offer workshops and bringing art.

## Shifts

Taking shifts is one of the most direct ways to embody the principle of **Participation** — and it's essential to making KiezBurn happen!

Here's an overview of some of the shifts you can take on during build, strike or the event itself:

* 🪚 **Build Help**
* 🦸 **Ranger Shifts**
* 💖 **Welfare** 
* 🍲 **Production Kitchen**

You'll find **many more roles** in the full list online. Some are more crafty others are social — there's something for every vibe and capacity.

📋 **Shift Info at the Gate:**\nWhen you arrive, you can check the shift schedule at the Gate for details and updates.

🔄 **Can't Make Your Shift?**\nIf you can't make it, it's your responsibility to find a replacement.

==👉== [==Sign Up for Volunteer Shifts==](https://signup.com/group/4415699080106)

## 🎭 Offer Something for the Program

Got a workshop, ritual, performance, or spontaneous brilliance you want to share with the community?

Whether you're a seasoned facilitator or just excited to offer something — you can sign up to be included in the official program.

==🗓️ **Deadline to sign up: July 7th**==\n==👉== [==Sign up to offer something for the program==](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfka6nhDvRrjv0dpLW2Mqa1M-kYSiyUQYKEHtdDntRm2RUiNg/viewform?usp=sf_link)

You can also bring spontaneous offerings.

## 💡 Bring art

Got a wild idea, a beautiful art piece, or an interactive experience you want to share?\nWhether you're part of a camp or a solo dreamer — **you're invited to bring it to KiezBurn!**

You can:

* ✨ Apply for **Dream Funding** to get support in making it happen
* 🌈 Or simply **bring your dream without funding** — self-funded projects are just as magical and welcome!

🗓️ **Funding Application Deadline: 22nd May**\n==👉== [==How to submit a Dream==](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/how-can-i-apply-for-dreams-funding-h5VZfqc96K)

## 🔧 Take on a realizer role

Help co-create the magic of Kiez Burn by stepping into one of the many available roles.\nThere's something for everyone — from **lead** and **co-lead** positions to smaller support roles.

Some roles start early in the year, others become more active closer to the event. You could be helping with **insurance**, **power and light**, **communication**, or one of many other crucial areas.

👉 [See available roles](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/leads-and-responsibilities-2025-OrzdIIieVV)

Not sure what a role involves?\nCheck out the [Playbooks](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/realizer-playbooks-yCXobWFOGm) to get an idea of how each role works and what's expected.

## 🏘️ Join the association 

Kiez Burn is more than a week long event. 

If you want to take part on a larger scale, you can do so by joining the e.V., being part of the assemblies and/or even becoming a boardie 👉 [here](https://mmm.kiezburn.org/membership-ev).
