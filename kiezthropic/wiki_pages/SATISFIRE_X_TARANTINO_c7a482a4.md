## Description


Dear Kiez Burn Dream Weavers,


We are SATISFIRE, a Berlin-based performance collective working at the intersection of contemporary theatre, fire arts, and pop-cultural mythologies.

Last year, we brought a raw experiment to the playa: SATISFIRE x TARANTINO — Spectacular Dance of Death. It was sparks and potential—intense, chaotic, alive. We watched the crowd's faces. We felt it clearly: this piece doesn't want to be polished. It wants to be reborn as cinema.

Now we seek fuel not for an "upgrade," but for a transformation.

## ***The Vision: Live Action Cinema Made of Fire***

Imagine stepping into a Tarantino film that breathes, burns, and happens around you in real-time. A revenge thriller told through flame rather than film—where non-linear chapters unfold in flickering sequences, sharp as razor edits, hot as muzzle flash. The audience doesn't watch the story; they piece it together while standing inside it, surrounded by precise flame work, striking silhouettes, and the atmospheric pressure of a cinematic score.

This is Version 2.0: not a better version of the same show, but the realization of the original vision we couldn't fully execute with year-one resources.


## **What We're Actually Building**

We're reinventing fire performance as immersive cinema. That means:

 • Cinematic scenography – Stage elements that function like living movie sets, transforming the space into chapters you walk between

 • Precision fire equipment – Tools that allow millisecond timing for "edits" between scenes, not just safety improvements

 • Costumes as character architecture – Silhouettes that read instantly from 50 meters, drawing you into archetypes before a single flame rises

 • Light design as narrative grammar – Golden-hour glows, stark noir shadows, blood-red gels that make the fire perform in dialogue with darkness

 • Theatrical presence at film-scale – Eight performers trained not just in technique, but in the timing, stillness, and explosive release of cinematic acting


## **Why Kiez Burn Is The Only Place For This Premiere**

We believe this genre fusion—cinematic fire theatre—doesn't exist yet because it requires a specific alchemy: a community that understands both - radical experimentation and precise execution; a space where "spectacle" and "story" hold equal weight; an audience ready to be immersed rather than entertained.

Kiez Burn is our home. It's where the first sparks flew. We want to gift this community the first iteration of a new art form: the moment where fire performance stops being a display of skill and becomes a medium for cinematic storytelling.

This Is About Transformation, Not Equipment


Your support doesn't buy us better gear. It buys us the time, materials, and technical precision to close the gap between "fire show" and "living movie." We're asking for the resources to rehearse with the rigor of film blocking, to light scenes with the drama of cinema, and to create an experience where people leave not saying "cool fire tricks," but "I just lived through a movie."


Eight humans. One collective breath. A burning narrative that wants to become unforgettable.

It would be our deep pleasure and honor to offer this transformation to the community that taught us how to burn with intention.

Thank you for holding space for dreams that demand reinvention.

With sparks in our eyes and a story ready to ignite,

SATISFIRE 🌶️

## Environmental Impact and LNT

We handle Leave No Trace with great care and awareness. As a fire show, some emissions through burned fuel are unavoidable, but all props, costumes, and materials are long-lasting, reusable, and used repeatedly in future performances. Apart from the fuel itself, our show produces virtually no waste, and we work consciously to keep our impact minimal.

## Safety and Accessibility 

We perform with fire props; safety is non-negotiable.

Requirements:

Audience barrier or safe distance in front of stage.

No public access to backstage or prop storage zones.

All emergency exits, extinguishers and pathways must remain accessible.

We operate with our own fire safety procedures and designated safety person.
