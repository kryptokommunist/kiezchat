# Checklist & FAQ for contributors

You want to make Kiez Burn magical with us but aren't sure where to start? 

This little FAQ is here to give you a gentle nudge in the right direction.

Let us help you find your path to make your contribution fun.


### Why ==contributing== in the first place?

When you got your ticket, you should've seen the **[Shift Sign‑Up](https://signup.com/Group/4415699080106/)**. Taking shifts isn't just "nice to have" — **contributors, realizers &** **volunteers are what make KiezBurn happen** (also check our [principles](https://mmm.kiezburn.org/principles)). No involvement of everyone, no event. It's the easiest way to meet people, and actually feel like a part of this burn community. If you can, **join Build and Strike,** too. That's where the real bonding happens.

You've probably joined **Discord** by now and thought: *"wow… that's a lot of channels."* Don't stress it. Just check the ones connected to your shifts. That's where you'll get the info you need.

**Quick note:** Discord is for organizing, so keep it KiezBurn-related. For hanging out and chatting, join the weekly meetups at the Burner Embassy. Details are in the Community Calendar on Discord or in the Telegram Newsletter ❤️ *(Text inspired from the Newsletter on the 25th of April 26)* 


### I have questions about the ==organization & wordings!==

If you're new to KiezBurn, you've probably seen terms like **Realizer**, **Shiftplan**, **Camps**, **Dreams**, or **Spark**. It can feel like a lot at first, and that's completely normal. KiezBurn has its own structure and vocabulary, and getting familiar with it helps things click quickly. If you've been to another burn, some of this may already feel familiar.

### Where can I ==sign up for shifts?==

For all KiezBurn–related shifts (Ranger, Effigy, Welfare, Gate, etc.), you can sign up [here](https://signup.com/Group/4415699080106/). You'll need an email address and your Discord name.

If you want to sign up for shifts within your camp, those are organized by the camps themselves. Please reach out to your camp lead.

### What if I have ==questions about a shift== before I want to sign up or after I signed up? 

* Start by checking the **Discord channel** for the area you're interested in (like LNT, welfare, ranger, water, effigy, etc.).
* Some areas list **contact details** directly in the [signup text](https://signup.com/Group/4415699080106/).
* You can **always** reach out to the **shift coordination realizer** on [Discord](https://discord.com/channels/930739383525572629/1471132823929880722) → channel "shift coordination" (Jenn, Fredrik & Geri), either directly in the liked channel, via PM, or on Telegram.
* There's also a **[realizer list](https://wiki.kiezburn.org/doc/realizers-and-responsibilities-2026-wkzxrLPdIb)** on the wiki with helpful info to point you to the right person.
* For **team specific info** there will be threads in this [discord](https://discord.com/channels/930739383525572629/1471120745726869544) channel.

### What if I need to ==change my shift== or ==can't show up==?

This happens. 

* Until 1 week before your shifts, you can simply change it via signup. 
* If it is closer to the date, please **try to find a replacement or if someone can switch with you** and 
* **let your shift lead/the realizer know directly.**

### Should I join a ==Camp== or ==freecamp==?

A **Kiez** is simply what we call a camp. Since this is the Berlin regional, we use the Berlin word. Each Kiez brings its own setup - music, workshops, chill spaces, or something totally different. Joining one means **co‑creating** and having a built‑in community.

**Want to join a Kiez?**   Some camps are still open. Check Discord or the [Kiez Kollection](https://mmm.kiezburn.org/2026-camps). There's also a **[Kiez Matchmaking](https://discord.com/channels/930739383525572629/1487732831906955394)** channel if you're looking for a good fit (Third Base optional).

You can also **Free Camp**. You'll still meet plenty of people by exploring, joining workshops, and taking shifts.

Just remember: **be fully self‑reliant**. Bring everything you need for the whole Burn (see the Survival Guide). Burns aren't festivals — **no commerce, no shops, nothing to buy on‑site**. *(Text inspired from the Newsletter on the 25th of April 26)*
