# 🥳 The Next Stage

The Next Stage is back! \nWe want to offer things that are either brain food (talks), funny moments (quiz, karaoke, useless talent show), or performances (cabaret, small concert), and quite often a mix of these. Some of the contents is directly made by us, but a lot of it come from other Kiez Burn participants, so we also offer the stage as place for expression.

Our stage is attached to a backstage that is entirely provided by the camp members. We also consider ways to make the audience area cosier and prettier - at our own cost.

## Description

The concept is basically the same as last year, the realization will be hopefully even nicer. The main changes to the stage is that the base will be higher, and the roof structure will be home-build rather than using an existing tent like last year. 

We haven't changed the dimensions of the stage, which allows us to reuse the stage floor (Wood + carpet), as well as the backdrop. The structure that we plan on buying this year will be entirely reusable in the coming years! 

## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

The Next Stage should be on the strip, where Hilton was last year, at the beginning of the semi-loud zone.

## Environmental Impact and LNT

Our stage is made of some elements of the stage 25 but better and a lil bigger. We aim at reusing all materials in the next years, until we take over the giant stage. We would make sure to gift the materials we can to emerging projects if that ever happens. \nOur backstage is only made of MOOP-free costumes, no glitter at the makeup station. We re/upcycle all that we can and will come up with trash-based deco elements to give it a better visual unity.
