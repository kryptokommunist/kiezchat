## Description

A Performance space run by Saunacious Spa.

🎭 **Platform for Performance**

A stage for the people, by the people! Kiezburn is burning with talent. Let's give them a beautiful stage to let loose! Theatre, stand-up, dance, live music - All have approached us already, offering to put on a spectacle. KB needs somewhere for all of these theatre kids and glee geeks to present themselves. I think they can put on the greatest show on earth (with or without substances)

🔥 **Artistic impact, unique**

It will be a real wonder. We have tools and expertise in our sauna community that enable us to build this thing big, beautiful, environmentally friendly, and long-lasting. The wood and structure choices will be useable for many years, and it will look fucking gorgeous. Moreover we are working beautifying some tarp walls and roof with impressive paint pattenrs in a classy golden hue this year. It will become even more mighty!

🎵 **Music (chill, sauna-appropriate)**

Many members wish for a bit of FKK dancing by the sauna space. Limited to 10pm for any real volume.

## Environmental Impact and LNT

As intended for its build last year, all the materials are re-usable and therefore will be used again this year. This year the focus lays on making more of a visual impact. Therfore we are working on decorations. Besides sourcing and working with "trash" materials such as PET / pfand bottles, we also want to work with paint. Additional fabrics are sourced second hand!

Once again all materials can reused in the coming years.

## Safety and Accessibility 

The columns creating the space were inspired by a madman, designed by a mechanical engineer, and checked by a carpenter and structural engineer. They are there for decoration, and to support the tarps for shade and shelter. If there is a collapse this would likely be controlled due to the many anchoring points, and (apart from some lights if deemed secure enough), the columns won't be taking any additional weight on the structure.

## Experience

It is the second time we are building this structure, so we will definitely set it up and work on improving the build process and making everything more shiny. 

Moreover, we have successfully run the performance space last year and already are working on a colourful program for this year.

For glorifying thr Colosseum walls and roof with classy golden paint, we have an experienced Deco Lead / aritst whose inspiration pictures are included. They will deliver!
