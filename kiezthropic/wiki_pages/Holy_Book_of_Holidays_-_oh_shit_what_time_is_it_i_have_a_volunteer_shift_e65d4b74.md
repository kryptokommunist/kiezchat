## Description

The Dream is to print a zine, which doubles as a calender and note taking space. Its a gift to be given out during Greetings. Since I'm part of the Romanescos, yes, it would be vegetable themed on the cover. The inside marks our Holy Holidays. TuTu Tuesday, Acid Friday, Burn Day, Temple Burn, etc. other fun gimmicks


My hope with this is to also remind Volunteers to write down their shifts these. Its small (folded to about 5cm in size, so easily stored in any pocket.
