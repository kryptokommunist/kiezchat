### **Long version:**

We're here for anyone having a difficult time — whether it's feelings, overwhelm, or a tricky moment with magic substances — and we offer a gentle, non‑judgmental space to land.

At the heart of our work is the Welfare Tent, a cozy hub where anyone can come for support. We'll also have Walking Care Bears roaming the burn, offering proactive care, grounding presence, and little moments of sweetness.

### 🌟 **Perks**

* The warm, fuzzy feeling of helping others feel safe, seen, and supported
* Being part of a space that genuinely makes people feel cared for 🐻

### 💚 **Requirements**

No prior experience needed. To join the crew, it is required that all potential Care Bears attend a training unless you bring already some experience. 

Please also register [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe6h_SiUHdYDrcFURaYBjPZcXzY3VjsgpYQAAvnZgoeDrhIYA/viewform) for the training.

### **Shift Descriptions**  

**🧸 Welfare Care Bear**

Support participants during the event. You'll be in the welfare tent for people who are having a hard time and need grounding, kindness, or a calm presence.

**🚶 Walking Care Bear**

Wander the burn with treats, small gifts, and a caring vibe. Offer proactive support and check in on people who might need a moment of connection.

**🌼 Welfare Lead**

Support the Care Bear team during their shift. You'll help coordinate shifts, check in on volunteers, document important happenings, and ensure communication flows smoothly. This shift is a bit longer, but you can take breaks as needed — as long as you remain reachable for the Care Bears.

🛠️ **Build & Strike** Help set up and take down the Welfare space. This includes building the tent, moving structures from storage, arranging mattresses, and adding decorations. You need to be comfortable with physical work and building tasks. Joining for a full day or two is super helpful.
