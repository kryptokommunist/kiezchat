## Safety and Accessibility 

Clear instructions are displayed on the outside of the Grabble Box. Hand Sanitizer is provided as well.

Participants inside actively choose to enter and can define the level of interaction. A visible occupancy light indicates when the box is in use. A panic button inside immediately signals (light + sound) that all interaction must stop.

The structure is stable, padded on the inside. It is mobile and placed on flat ground; access is simple, though not fully barrier-free.
