# Welcome to the Kiez Burn Survival Guide

This survival guide is a kind of special document. It's the digital version of a pamphlet photocopied throughout the years of Kiez Burn, written by different people, with sections torn out and others pasted in. It's chaotic and overwhelming, and we hope in that, it helps you prepare for Kiez Burn.

Some parts of the text is ==highlighted in yellow== to indicate that we're not yet sure if the information is accurate for this year. Other information that's also not accurate for this year is likely available without this highlight too. You never know what you get but sometimes what you get is just get the exact right thing (and some things here are pretty darn accurate, too.)

If you're uncertain about something, it's always best to ask. And the best place to ask is in [our Discord server (join via the Portal).](https://portal.kiezburn.org/join/930739383525572632)
