## Description

This year's Temple is imagined as a space for moments that dissolve into silence: contemplation and overwhelm, grief and awe, loss and hope, love that has nowhere to go, moments that require no words, explanations or purpose, the ying to all the party yang.

Within this year's theme, Diffusion, the Tower of Silence is not about disappearance from the world but about a shift in state, when we find ourselves more connected to what is happening within us. This movement continues in the form: The tower rises into something lighter, less fixed, gradually losing itself in the sky and trees.

In the spirit of Temple building at the Big Burns, Tower of Silence is conceived not as a finished object handed down from above, but as a shared structure that can receive many hands and visions. Like last year, it will take shape through the contributions of different artists. As such, it is meant as a collective gift to the community: a space we build together and offer to hold all those things that cannot be explained away.
