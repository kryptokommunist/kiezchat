## Description

Kiez Burn deserves some voguish haute couture and name a more chic garment than a bandana? This year, the Volunteer Coordination Team wants to give every volunteer the possibility to manufacture and design a bandana that they can wear to proudly and fashionably communicate that they are taking valuable Kiez Burn shifts. They will also be lasting memories when the burn is over. We will create the bandanas during our volunteer & realizer picnic on the 24th of May.

**Bonus add-ons: Can lids and screen printing**

To make the SWAG package even sweeter, two additions were proposed. Both would use our beautiful, trippy KB-26 logo. 

 ![](/api/attachments.redirect?id=0ee2fdae-09da-4fe4-8049-728bd6d03e33 " =300x212")

* 3D-printed can lids designed by Tacha. Pop them on open cans to protect from dust and bees.
* Screen prints. Print the logo on our bandanas and mono-colored pieces of clothing.

We'll provide more details on the add-ons later.
