## Description

The effigy has burned, but the fire still rages in the hearts of the burners. For those whose fire smokes darkly, we offer a witches' revelry: a night of sex magic, potions, and music that reverberates in the darkest depths of our souls. A ceremonial hut will host the sex magic: consecrated and blessed, glowing with intention, bedazzled with relics. An aphrodesiac bar will offer a variety of herbal tonics that delight the lips, flush the cheeks and open the heart. A mysterious berry will be offered that casts spells upon the tongue, making the most sour of lemons somehow sweet. A buffet of bitter delights will allow those enchanted by the berry to explore the consequences of their syrupy entrapment.

The evening will begin with a live performance of enchanted ambient loop music put on by the beautiful and legendary Amanda Meow, along with an intentions ceremony performance. Following will be a goth DJ set by Kardinal Kunt Juan, and Lena shall bring the night to a climax with her occult beats.

In the case of not finding a home for the music, we will downsize that aspect as necessary.

## Existing funds

The sex magic ceremonial hut will be a 30 sq m teepee, and has been purchased by Lee. They also intend to purchase consumables for the event. The Harlots do not have programming funds this year. Funds that cannot be provided by the Dream will hopefully be raised by the orgas.


## Safety and Accessibility 

The sex magic hut will essentially function as a darkroom. We will have a list of consent rules on the door and an age limit sign.

The tent is a mainstream tent.

No candles or incense will burn indoors without sober supervision. Outdoor candles will only be at the potions' station and will be monitored by aphrodesiac bartenders.
