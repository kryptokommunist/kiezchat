## Description

### **The dream is a letter and postcard writing station!** 


Do you miss sending and receiving postcards and always thought of doing it but never found the time or occasion ?  That's when you burst into the Postcard station "From Burn with Love". Take a comfortable seat, choose a postcard - or make your own- and only focus on writing.. We take care of the rest ! 


**How it works:**

Burners can come around and design their own postcard by using drawing or collage. They can also pick a ready postcard from the station and simply write a message. They can also write paper letters up to 20g and enclose them in an envelope. They need to write the name and address of the addressee but they don't need to add the postage (no risk of forgetting to post the card).The organisers of the station will collect the finished letters and postcards, stick the post stamps and send them in the mail after the Burn. 

In the station you find different tools and materials, from paper to collage supplies, wheat glue, and postcard bases. 

Write away! Declare your love! Send a greeting from Kiezburn to your grandma !

!!It is obviously the best quality dirt one can find in and on the region, and will be used year over year.!!
