## Description

The U7 is driving around the Kiez Burn according to the VeryAccurate™ Fahrplan on the U7 Long\*est Line™. We will be transporting burners to both physically and psychedelically to places. Einstein Bitte! But don't forget your ticket!

We are dreaming to make some upgrades to our intergalactic public transporter! Firstly - we would like to build a new roof to make U7 more comfortable and safe\*r during rain. Secondly we would like to fluff the insides and make the structure a bit more sturdy so that it will endure all the adventured that the travellers take it to.


## Where at Kiezburn would you like your Dream/ Art to be?

The U7 would move on the concrete strip. When used - parked in front of the BVG camp.

## Existing funds

We had a dream last year to finance the purchase of the base of the U7. It's fully built, largely of recycled materials. We are also bringing lights and battery to power them. 

## Safety and Accessibility 

It's a 4 person bicycle. Anyone who can pedal can move it. It stands on its own and it's not easy to flip over. By keeping the speed low the safety risks are low. 

## Experience

We are experienced dreamers and the U7 has already been to 2 events. 

## Power usage estimate

We have a battery system and only need to charge the batteries (will do in our camp)
